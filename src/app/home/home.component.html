Atacama Manufacturing

Atacama is an innovative and fast-growing manufacturer of plant-based and reusable plastic products based in the USA. The company has the capacity to design, formulate, manufacture and distribute environmentally friendly products.

The Urgent Need to Solve the Global Plastic Waste Problem

Consumers

Consumers around the world are demanding more environmentally friendly products and solutions.

Legislation

Regions of the world like the EU are legislating that single use plastics must be reduced or eliminated.

Waste

Half of all plastics ever manufactured have been made in the last 15 years.

Production

Plastics production increased exponentially, from 2.3 million tons in 1950 to 448 million tons by 2015. Production is expected to double by 2050.

Raw Material Innovation

What makes Atacama unique is an integrated approach to manufacturing. We create unique and novel bioplastic raw materials that enable enhanced performance and compostability capabilities.

Disruptive Product Development

The key to rapid time to market is having the right tools and structure to enable collaboration between a customer and their supplier. Atacama has over thirty years’ experience in plastic part and tool design. We can support customers with full product designs or collaborate using our CADD tools to enable manufacturable designs.

Manufacturing

We manufacture plant-based, marine safe and home compostable products as well as reusable products.

Our capabilities include:

  • Raw Material Formulation
  • Product Design & Development
  • Extrusion / Injection Molding / Thermoforming
  • Bioplastic and reusable product manufacturing
  • Advanced Robotics & Automation

R&D activities and Manufacturing are located in Oregon, USA.

Circular Economy

Atacama is also developing technologies and partnerships in the Circular Economy. A successful pilot project has been completed with a major national restaurant chain. We manufacture plant-based plastic products, collect them after use and remanufacture new products.

Over 30,000 lbs of plant based plastic have been reclaimed and remanufactured to date.

CHRIS VITELLO
CEO/FOUNDER

Chris Vitello founded Atacama Manufacturing with a vision for creating innovative raw materials and manufacturing solutions to reduce waste and improve the planet for future generations. Chris possesses a BS in Plastics Engineering from the University of Massachusetts – Lowell.
