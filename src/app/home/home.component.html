Atacama Manufacturing

Atacama is an innovative and fast-growing manufacturer of plant-based and reusable plastic products based in the USA. The company has the capacity to design, formulate, manufacture and distribute these products.

Atacama's Mission is to Address Global Plastic Waste

Consumers

Consumers around the world are demanding more sustainable products and solutions.

Legislation

Regions of the world like the EU are legislating restrictions on single use plastics.

Waste

Landfill site costs are increasing and availability is decreasing.

Demand

Demand for plastics has increased production from 2.3 million tons in 1950 to 448 million tons in 2015.

Raw Material Innovation

What makes Atacama unique is an integrated approach to manufacturing. We create unique and novel bioplastic proprietary material blends that enable enhanced performance and compostability capabilities.

Circular Economy

Atacama is also developing technologies and partnerships in the Circular Economy. A successful pilot project has been completed with a major national restaurant chain. We manufacture plant-based plastic products, collect them after use and remanufacture new products.

Over 30,000 lbs of plant based plastic have been reclaimed and remanufactured to date.
