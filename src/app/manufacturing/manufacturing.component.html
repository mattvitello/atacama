Injection Molding

Atacama has over thirty years experience in low and high volume injection molding production. We specialize in rapid prototype tooling development and close collaboration with our customers to ensure designs are manufacturable and products meet quality expectations. Our capabilities include single cavity tooling and low volume production through high volume manufacturing and assembly.

Worldwide tooling and manufacturing facilities provide customers with the ability to optimize the cost and lead time for their projects. Tooling and manufacturing facilities in Ningbo China augment US based tooling and molding facilities in Corvallis, Oregon.

Extrusion

Atacama has expertise and capability to cost effectively custom develop material formulas for customer projects. Whether the goal is to reduce cost or optimize properties we have the ability to create custom formulas and feed them into our manufacturing processes to produce components. This is a distinct advantage for our customers as it great reduces time to market and enables product solutions which can not be obtained with off the shelf materials.

Thermoforming

Atacama Ningbo has a state of the art thermforming operation capable of producing high volume thermforming products for customers. Atacama Corvallis has prototype and low volume thermoforming capabilities to assist customers with product development and low volume production.

Pre-Production Manufacturing

Atacama utilizes 3-D printing to enable rapid design evolution which saves considerable time to market and cost on the front end of a project. Rapid prototype tooling is typically constructed in 5-6 week lead-times which enables the production of low volume products for customers. Production tooling can be enabled locally in the US or Asia dependent on economics or customer preference. (See Tool Construction in US & Asia for additional information)

High volume production

Atacama produces 30 million components per year our US manufacturing facility and 100+ million in our Asia facilities. Our culture is built around quality and continuous improvement. Through strong engineering principles and tools and an effective quality system we ensure customers expectations are met or exceeded.

Tool construction

Atacama's US tooling partners build high quality single and multi-cavity tooling for customers. Cold and hot runner tooling can be constructed and our focus is to provide the highest level of quality and service possible.

A capable network of Asia tooling suppliers has been assembled which provide high quality tooling at exceptional prices to customers. Customers have the choice of building tools in Asia and running the product in the US or building and running parts in Asia.
